The TechGuard Laptop Bag was a huge disappointment. Despite its claims of durability, the zippers feel flimsy and snag easily, and the compartments are poorly designed for organization. The so-called anti-theft features seem gimmicky, and the RFID pocket barely fits standard cards. The USB charging port is more hassle than help, and for $85, the overall quality feels cheap. I wouldn’t recommend this bag to anyone.
