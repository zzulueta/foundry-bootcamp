# Contoso Coffee Company - Customer Support FAQ

## Technical Support

### My coffee machine won't turn on. What should I do?
- Ensure the power cord is firmly connected at both ends.
- Try a different power outlet.
- Unplug the machine for 30 seconds, then plug it back in.
- Check for any lights or sounds when pressing the power button.
- If the issue persists, contact support with your model number.

### My coffee machine displays 'Error Code E47'.
- Error Code E47 indicates a water flow issue.
- Check that the water reservoir is filled and properly seated.
- Remove and clean the water reservoir, then reseat it.
- Replace the water filter if needed.
- If the error persists, the pump may need servicing.

### The Wi-Fi connectivity keeps dropping on my smart coffee maker.
- Ensure your router's 2.4GHz band is enabled (machine does not support 5GHz).
- Restart your coffee maker and router.
- Place the router closer to the machine.
- Check for firmware updates in the Contoso app.

### My machine is leaking water from the bottom.
- Turn off and unplug the machine immediately.
- Check that the drip tray and water reservoir are properly positioned.
- Inspect for visible cracks.
- If the leak persists, contact support for service or replacement (may be covered under warranty).

### How do I descale my coffee machine?
- Use Contoso descaling solution or white vinegar.
- Run a descaling cycle (see your model's manual or settings menu).
- Run 2-3 rinse cycles with fresh water after descaling.
- Descale every 3 months with regular use.

## Product Information

### What's the difference between the Pro and Premium coffee machine models?
- **Pro:** Manual controls, standard water reservoir.
- **Premium:** Larger water reservoir, built-in grinder, Wi-Fi/app control.
- Choose based on desired features and budget.

### Does the Contoso Deluxe model support both ground coffee and pods?
- Yes, it supports both ground coffee and compatible pods.
- Includes separate brew chambers for each type.

### What's the warranty period for Contoso coffee machines?
- Standard 2-year manufacturer's warranty for all models.
- Premium and Deluxe models include an extended 3-year warranty.
- Warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship (not normal wear and tear).

## Billing & Subscription

### When will my monthly subscription charge appear on my credit card?
- Charged on the same date each month as your original sign-up date.
- Check your account settings for your next billing date.

### I was charged twice for the same order. What should I do?
- Contact support with your order number, charge date, and amount.
- Support will verify and refund the duplicate charge (refunds process in 3-5 business days).

### Can I upgrade my subscription from Basic to Premium mid-cycle?
- Yes, upgrades are allowed at any time.
- You will be charged a prorated amount for the Premium features for the remainder of your current cycle.

### I noticed a strange charge labeled 'Contoso Premium Plus'.
- Contoso Premium Plus is an optional service tier with premium delivery and extended warranty.
- Contact support to verify or dispute the charge.

### Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
- Yes, you can cancel at any time with no cancellation fees.
- Service continues until the end of your current billing period.
